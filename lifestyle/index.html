Slower

I often look at time as something fast. Things happen in the blink of an eye. Different situations form in front of you and then disappear regardless of how you react to them. But, a few days ago, for a moment, I felt it is also really slow. And it gets slower when you limit your thoughts and actions. You feel you have more space, and you can breathe. In other words, the amount of “things” you do and think about, has a direct effect on your feeling of the speed of time.
