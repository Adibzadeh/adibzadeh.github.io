Springtime

Mar 13, 2021

I’m glad that springtime is coming.

Spring is good and refreshing. I believe everything about it is very standard and normal. Although Summer is still my favorite season, Spring is the second one. And there is one small reason for it called “Hope”. I feel It’s like the planet wakes up after weeks of hard and cold days. And you can see it’s still hopeful, green and fresh!

Also, Spring is the start of a new Iranian year. Despite 2020, which was not a very good year, I have a lot of hope for the upcoming year. Mostly because I learned a lot from my last job, I’m ready to work in a more professional environment and I know a lot more about teamwork. All of these give me the confidence to move one step closer to the point I want to be.

The walking is also great, today was a very snowy and cold day but (actually, I don’t know why!) this kind of day feels a lot better for walking. I get less tired and I can walk more. I hope there be a lot of rainy days in the Spring!

Have a lovely day.
see ya.
